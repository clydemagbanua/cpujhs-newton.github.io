Mafia III

Mafia III is a 2016 action-adventure video game developed by Hangar 13 and published by 2K Games. It is a action-adventure set in an open world and played from a third-person perspective. Set within the fictional city of New Bordeaux (based on New Orleans) in 1968, the story follows former criminal and Vietnam veteran Lincoln Clay, who is forced to return to a life of crime to help his adoptive family settle problems with the local branch of the Mafia.


Assassin's Creed III


Assassin's Creed III is a 2012 action-adventure video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal.The plot is set in a fictional history of real-world events and follows the millennia-old struggle between the Assassins, who fight to preserve peace and free will, and the Templars, who desire peace through control. The framing story is set in the 21st century and features series protagonist Desmond Miles

Watch Dogs 2

Watch Dogs 2 is a 2016 action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft Montreal. Set within a fictionalized version of the San Francisco Bay Area, the game is played from a third-person perspective and its open world is navigated on-foot or by vehicle. Players control Marcus Holloway, a hacker who works with the hacking group DedSec to take down the city's advanced surveillance system known as ctOS.
